CHITCHAT PAYS SERVICES GO GLOBAL

To provide fast, secure and reliable worldwide payments, ChitChat Pays uses a Virtual Bank that connects with local mobile money and banking services in countries outside the United States.

Every user is required to activate their account before becoming eligible to receive payments. Activation enables your Virtual Bank and ensures that your earnings are securely stored and managed.

Your Earnings

  • All earnings are automatically credited to your Virtual Bank.
  • Your available balance is visible anytime inside your ChitChat account.
  • Withdrawals can be made once every 7 days.
  • Once approved, your withdrawal is instantly sent to your linked mobile money number.
  • Please ensure your registered mobile number is correct to avoid payment delays.
